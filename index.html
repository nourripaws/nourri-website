Our Process

From kitchen to your dog's bowl.

Every batch follows a strict 4-step process — fresh sourcing to SFA-compliant post-heat treatment. No shortcuts, ever.

01

Source Fresh Daily

Chicken, salmon and fresh produce sourced from Singapore's wet markets — the same places you shop for your own family.

02

Mix & Mould

Fresh ingredients blended into a nutritious paste, pressed into hemisphere moulds with real botanical decorations on top.

03

Low-Temp Dehydrate

10–14 hours at 63–65°C preserves nutrients and natural colours while building a long-lasting, satisfying chew texture.

04

SFA Post-Heat

135°C oven for 10 minutes eliminates all pathogens — Salmonella, E. coli, Listeria. Mandatory. Non-negotiable. Always.
