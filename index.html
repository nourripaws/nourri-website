How It's Made

From kitchen
to your dog's bowl.

Every batch follows a strict 4-step process. No shortcuts, ever.

🛒
Step 01

Source Fresh Daily

Chicken and fresh produce sourced from Singapore's wet markets — the same places you shop for your own family.

🫙
Step 02

Mix & Mould

Blended into a nutritious paste, pressed into hemisphere moulds with real botanical decorations pressed on top.

🌡️
Step 03

Low-Temp Dehydrate

10–14 hours at 63–65°C preserves nutrients and natural colours while building a long-lasting chew texture.

✅
Step 04

SFA Post-Heat

135°C for 10 minutes eliminates all pathogens — Salmonella, E. coli, Listeria. Mandatory. Non-negotiable.
